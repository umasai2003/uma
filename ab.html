AMAZON PRIME

In 2005, Amazon announced Amazon Prime as a membership service offering free two-day shipping within the contiguous United States on all eligible purchases for an annual fee of $79 (equivalent to $118 in 2022)[4] and discounted one-day shipping rates.[5] Amazon launched the program in Germany, Japan and the United Kingdom in 2007; in France (as "Amazon Premium") in 2008, in Italy in 2011, in Canada in 2013,[6] in India in July 2016,[7] in Mexico in March 2017,[8] in Turkey in September 2020,[9] in Sweden in September 2021,[10] in Poland in October 2021,[11] and in Egypt in July 2022.[12] Amazon Prime is also available in Ireland, with a new fulfilment centre having opened in Dublin in 2022.[2] As of October 2021, there are Prime members in 22 countries in North America, Europe and
